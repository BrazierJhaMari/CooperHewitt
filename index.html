Controlling the Future
What story does it tell about the community’s history and identity?

Each generation of controllers improves comfort, customization, and accessibility. New layouts, adaptive features, and feedback options help more people play comfortably. This evolution shows a community that values inclusion and progress, reflecting a shared belief that gaming should be enjoyable and accessible for everyone.


Tell us about your design and the community members or group you are designing for. Could this design have a broader impact, helping communities across the United States?


Many players don’t feel comfortable with standard controllers, especially those with dexterity challenges. Controllers often assume everyone interacts the same way, but real players bring a wide range of abilities and play styles. This effort focuses on creating flexible, adaptive ways to play by drawing inspiration from the customized setups many people already use. The goal is to build a supportive space where players can shape their own configurations, share solutions, and learn from one another. By empowering individuals to play in ways that feel natural to them, we strengthen accessibility and expand who gets to participate. This approach helps make gaming more welcoming and enjoyable for communities across the United States.
